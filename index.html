Giving Technology a Second Life

SecondStepTech collects, refurbishes, and redistributes used technology to those who need it most. Join us in reducing e-waste and bridging the digital divide.

Learn How to Donate

Our Mission

At SecondStepTech, we believe technology should empower everyone. Many functional devices end up in landfills while many people lack access to basic tech tools. We're changing that by giving technology a second step in its lifecycle.

SecondStepTech refurbishes donated technology and puts it in the hands of those who need it most. Join us in bridging the digital divide one device at a time.

Our Mission

At SecondStepTech, we believe in the power of technology to transform lives. Our mission is to collect, refurbish, and redistribute technology to underserved communities, schools, and individuals who lack access to digital resources.

Our Impact

Since our founding, we've refurbished over hundreds of devices and helped countless individuals gain access to technology they otherwise couldn't afford. Each device we save from a landfill is another opportunity for someone to learn, connect, and grow.

About SecondStepTech

SecondStepTech was founded with a simple idea: give used technology a second step in its lifecycle while helping bridge the digital divide. We collect donated devices, refurbish them, and distribute them to those in need.
